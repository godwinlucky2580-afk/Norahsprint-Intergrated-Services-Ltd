Who We Are

Built on Craft,
Driven by Excellence

NorahsPrint Integrated Services is a Port Harcourt-based multi-service company with over a decade of excellence in premium furniture craftsmanship, certified electrical installations, and professional & Pop solutions.

We source the finest African hardwoods and local materials to create statement pieces that reflect Nigeria's rich heritage. Our engineers are fully certified and bring international standards to every project — from luxury homes to large commercial builds across the Niger Delta region.
